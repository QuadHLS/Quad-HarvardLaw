This Privacy Policy explains how Quad collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards personal information when you use the Service. By creating an account, signing in, or using the Service, you agree to this Policy and to the Terms of Service. This Policy applies to information we collect through the Service and our communications with you. It does not apply to thirdparty websites or services we do not control.

What We Collect (Categories of Personal Information)

  • Identifiers & Contact Data: name; email; optional phone number.
  • Academic/Enrollment Data: classes and extracurricular groups you designate as “enrolled” (Directory Information).
  • Account & Authentication Data: credentials; verification signals (e.g., association with @law.harvard.edu).
  • UserGenerated Content: Student Materials you upload (outlines, prior exam answers you authored, professor reviews).
  • Usage/Device Data: IP address; device/browser type; pages viewed; timestamps; and cookie/analytics data necessary to operate, secure, and improve the Service.

Uploaded files may contain personal data (e.g., your name in headers/metadata). Remove data you do not wish to disclose before uploading. Quad is not responsible for personal data you include in uploads.

Sources

  1. You, when you register, configure your profile (including classes), or upload Student Materials.
  2. Automatic collection via the Service (e.g., cookies/analytics).
  3. Identity/eligibility verification (e.g., SSO/email domain checks).

How We Use Personal Information (Purposes)

We use personal information to: (a) provide, operate, and secure the Service (account creation, HLS verification, hosting Student Materials, enforcing Terms); (b) display your Directory Information (name and enrolled classes) to other verified HLS users inside the Service; emails/phone numbers are not displayed; (c) communicate with you (service notices, updates); (d) improve the Service (analytics, debugging, abuse prevention); (e) comply with law (e.g., DMCA repeatinfringer policy) and enforce our Terms.

What Other Users See

  • Shown to other verified HLS users: your name and enrolled classes (Directory Information), and any author name you include in your Student Materials.
  • Not shown: your email and phone number (unless you place them inside an uploaded file or expressly authorize display).

Disclosures (Recipients)

We disclose personal information only to: (a) Service providers/contractors (hosting, storage, analytics, security, email delivery) under written restrictions; (b) Other verified HLS users, solely as to Directory Information and Student Materials you choose to post; (c) Legal/compliance recipients (e.g., pursuant to law, subpoena, or to enforce rights); (d) Business transferees in a merger, acquisition, or similar event (with notice where required).

We do not sell personal information and do not “share” it for crosscontext behavioral advertising. If our practices change, we will update this Policy and provide required rights/optouts.

Cookies & Analytics

We use strictly necessary cookies to operate and secure the Service and limited analytics cookies to understand usage and improve features. You can control cookies via your browser; disabling some cookies may affect functionality. We may use reputable analytics services consistent with this Policy.
